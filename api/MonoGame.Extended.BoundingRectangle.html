Remarks

An BoundingRectangle is categorized by having its faces oriented in such a way that its face normals are at all times parallel with the axes of the given coordinate system.

The BoundingRectangle of a rotated BoundingRectangle will be equivalent or larger in size than the original depending on the angle of rotation.
