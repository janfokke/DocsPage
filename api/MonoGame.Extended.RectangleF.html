Struct RectangleF

An axis-aligned, four sided, two dimensional box defined by a top-left position (X and Y) and a size (Width and Height).

Remarks

An RectangleF is categorized by having its faces oriented in such a way that its face normals are at all times parallel with the axes of the given coordinate system.

The bounding RectangleF of a rotated RectangleF will be equivalent or larger in size than the original depending on the angle of rotation.
